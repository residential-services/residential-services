01.

Receiving announcements

The company that manages the building you live in can send you announcements that have time information attached to them. An announcement can pertain to an event such as a repair job or a local gathering for example. You can read relevant announcements with the Equa Phi app.

02.

Receiving documents

Your property manager can send you documents, and you can access them using the Aqua Phi app. A document can be a lease document or a house rules document for example.
